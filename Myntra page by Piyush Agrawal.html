    BUY SHIRTS FOR MEN
    SIMPLE YET STYLISH - THE BASICS OF SHIRTS.

    If you’re thinking men are short of choices when it comes to clothes, think again. T-shirts surely work as the most practical outfit for men but the classic men’s shirt is far more suave. The most basic of men’s shirts can be styled in a multitude of ways to look visibly different but keeping true to the function of the outfit. The options for styles are vast, you can pick the style of placket, collar, cuffs, sleeves, back pleat, hemline and much more. Shirts of today have certainly come a long way from the baggy fits of the past decades. If you’re looking to add some trendy shirts or kurtas to your wardrobe, start your search at Myntra in the styles mentioned below.

    While many would prefer to get a tailor made shirt for the best fit; finding a skilled tailor, the right fabric and the long wait between fabric to final product is expensive and not always feasible. Brands today offer a variety of fits to suit every body frame and it’s only best to buy mens shirts online.

    From the wide range of shirts for men available online, here are the top choices for every closet.

    Solid Shirts: Solids are the identity of the straightforward man; and the perfect partner for a formal blazer and classic leather shoes. Defined as the quintessential corporate look, solids are the epitome of minimalism, the flat single colour stands out brilliantly in a crowd of prints and stripes. Solids paired with formal trousers and shoes suit every occasion and also allow a variety of choices in colour from mild cool shades to bright standout ones. Perfect your look by wearing the right colour at the right place.

    Chequered Shirts:Certainly one of the best shirts for men and the signature of the typical guy next door, chequered shirts are associated with men of a friendly personality and easy going attitude. The relaxed look of these shirts gives out a very country vibe, one of relaxation and taking things at a slow pace. Chequered shirts can make you look wider, find the right fit and go for it. The rule to follow with these shirts is darker shirts for the evening and brighter colours for the day.

    Printed Shirts: Designer shirts for men are dominated by prints and patterns. Prints on shirts bring a delightful spark of life to the otherwise morose men’s wardrobe. Myntra offers a copious range of designers shirts to let you stand out from the rest. Go crazy with prints, from geometric patterns to abstract art, printed shirts are perfect for the fun loving man, who is the life of the party.

    Striped Shirts: Rightfully called the unsung hero of our wardrobes, we’ve all been saved by the striped shirt, in those last minute rushes to pick something nice to wear. Famed for its versatility, it can double from a formal outfit to causal one by just rolling up the sleeves. The trendy stripes are a beloved fashion favourite and will always hold its place as a closet essential.

    There is an unmatched elegance in a man who is dressed in a crisp shirt, well ironed formal trousers and tidy formal shoes. So find your favourite fabric and pick your fit carefully, and choose a style that fits your body frame. A free size could leave you floating and a slim fit could make you feel uneasy. Never over accessorize with printed shirts, just a simple bomber jacket and sneakers would complete the look.

    Your shirt defines you, so always wear it tastefully. Start your shopping on Myntra today, the best place for men’s shirts online in India.
